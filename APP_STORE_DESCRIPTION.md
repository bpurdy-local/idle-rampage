## Description

**RISE AGAINST THE MACHINES**

In a world overrun by rogue robots, you are humanity's last hope. Build a base from salvaged scrap, train survivors to fight, and push back against endless waves of hostile machines.

**BUILD YOUR EMPIRE**
Construct and upgrade 6 unique buildings, each evolving through multiple tiers:
- Scrap Works: Generate resources to fuel your operation
- Weak Point Scanner: Reveal enemy vulnerabilities for devastating critical hits
- Training Facility: Transform survivors into elite warriors
- Shield Generator: Buy precious extra time in battle
- Command Center: Coordinate your entire operation
- Engineering Bay: Reduce upgrade costs through innovation

**FIGHT THROUGH 100 WAVES**
Battle increasingly powerful robot enemies across 5 tiers - from lowly Scrap Bots to terrifying AI Units. Every 10 waves, face a powerful boss. Reach wave 100 to challenge The Architect, the ultimate test of your skills.

**TAP TO VICTORY**
Tap enemies to deal damage! Train your warriors and discover weak points to multiply your power. Trigger devastating burst attacks for massive damage spikes.

**PRESTIGE FOR POWER**
Reset your progress to earn Blueprints - a permanent currency that unlocks powerful upgrades. Each prestige cycle makes you stronger, helping you push further than before.

**NEVER STOP PROGRESSING**
Even while away, your base keeps working. Return to collect up to 8 hours of offline earnings. Daily login rewards keep you coming back for more.

**DISCOVER THE STORY**
Unlock building storylines as you evolve your base. Learn the history of the Collapse and humanity's fight for survival.

**FEATURES**
- 6 buildings with 4-5 evolution tiers each
- 100 waves of challenging combat
- 10 prestige upgrades for permanent power
- Worker management with strategic depth
- Lucky drops and daily rewards
- Offline progression
- No forced ads
- Regular updates planned

Download now and start your rampage!
