# Privacy Policy for Idle Rampage

**Last Updated: December 1, 2024**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Idle Rampage ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Game Progress**: Your game state, including resources, buildings, wave progress, and prestige upgrades, is stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: We may collect basic device information for crash reporting and analytics purposes.
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous gameplay statistics may be collected to improve game balance and user experience.

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not store your personal game data on external servers.

### Local Storage
- Game save data
- Daily reward tracking
- Settings preferences

## Third-Party Services

### In-App Purchases
If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Game data remains on your device unless you choose to export it.

## Children's Privacy

Idle Rampage is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Data Retention

Game data is stored on your device until you uninstall the app or manually clear the app data. You can export your save data at any time through the Settings menu.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your game data (via the export feature)
- Delete your game data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data)
- Reset your game progress (via the Settings menu)

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your game data stored locally on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
